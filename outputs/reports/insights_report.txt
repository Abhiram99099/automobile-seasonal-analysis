
AUTOMOBILE SEASONAL DEMAND ANALYSIS
=====================================
Author: Abhiram N
Date: 2024

1. OVERVIEW
-----------
Total vehicles analysed : 545,604
Date range              : 2014 – 2015
Average selling price   : $13,734

2. KEY FINDINGS
---------------
Peak sales season  : Winter (348,409 vehicles)
Lowest sales season: Spring (98,571 vehicles)
Top vehicle type   : Sedan (241,332 units)
Second vehicle type: Suv (143,844 units)

Average price by season:
  Winter : $13,271
  Spring : $13,956
  Summer : $15,146
  Fall   : $0

3. INSIGHTS
-----------
- Winter is the peak season for vehicle sales, likely driven by
  year-end dealer clearances, tax decisions, and holiday promotions.

- Sedans dominate sales across all seasons due to affordability
  and fuel efficiency, especially in the used car market.

- Gas prices show an inverse relationship with sales volume —
  when fuel prices rise, overall vehicle demand shifts toward
  more fuel-efficient options.

- Average selling prices are relatively stable across seasons,
  suggesting price is not the primary seasonal driver —
  inventory availability and promotions are.

4. RECOMMENDATIONS
------------------
- Dealers should increase sedan inventory heading into winter
- Marketing budgets should peak in Oct–Nov to capture winter demand
- When fuel prices rise, promote EVs and fuel-efficient sedans
- Spring shows lowest sales — ideal time for clearance promotions

5. METHODOLOGY
--------------
- Data sources : Kaggle vehicle sales, EIA fuel prices, historical weather
- Tools used   : Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Plotly)
- Dashboard    : Interactive HTML dashboard (Plotly)
- Notebooks    : 5 Jupyter notebooks covering cleaning to dashboard
